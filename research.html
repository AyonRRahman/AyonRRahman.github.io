Research Vision

My research focuses on building intelligent perception systems that can operate in real-world environments. I am particularly interested in combining deep learning with robotics to enable robust scene understanding, localization, and decision-making under challenging conditions such as underwater and industrial environments.

Future Research Directions

I aim to explore scalable multimodal AI systems, self-supervised learning for robotics, and real-time perception for autonomous systems. My long-term goal is to develop generalizable AI systems that can adapt to unseen environments with minimal supervision.
